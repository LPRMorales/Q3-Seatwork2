Project Sekai Wiki - Bands

Wonderlands x Showtime

An eccentric musical show group whose performances are always filled with lively, joyful musicals, making sure it fits everyone's preferences, aiming to make them smile one way or another.

Vivid BAD SQUAD

A group of talented performers who banded together to surpass a legendary event known as RAD WEEKEND. Their performances emphasize the diversity and uniqueness of hip-hop, teen pop, electronic, and street music, hoping to spread it wherever they perform.

MORE MORE JUMP!

A unique idol group formed by one girl with a dream of becoming a professional idol, and three other girls who had given up on their dream of becoming professional idols, but was later revived when the group was formed. Their performances focus on upbeat, j-pop styled songs, aiming to inspire others to pursue their dreams with their songs.

Nightcord at 25:00

A mysterious group that operates at 1:00 AM (25:00). Their performances focus and emphasize the immense negative view others' perceive about life and how they can feel misunderstood and isolated. Although their songs are quite negative and depressing, it can strangely bring a sense of comfort by means of relation to their listeners.
